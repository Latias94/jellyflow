# JVGP-020 - Headless Policy Proof

Status: DONE
Date: 2026-06-01

## Summary

Added the first renderer-neutral viewport gesture policy slice in
`crates/jellyflow-runtime/src/runtime/viewport.rs`.

The new policy types and resolvers cover:

- normalized wheel/trackpad scroll input;
- normalized pointer drag-pan input;
- user-selection and connection-in-progress rejection;
- pan-on-scroll mode and speed;
- zoom activation and Ctrl/pinch priority;
- allowed pointer buttons for drag-pan.

passing.
