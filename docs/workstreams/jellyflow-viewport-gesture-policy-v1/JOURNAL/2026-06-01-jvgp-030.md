Status: DONE

## Summary

Added renderer-neutral conformance actions for viewport gesture policy:

- accepted scroll gesture actions resolve policy and apply pan/zoom through `NodeGraphStore`;
- accepted drag-pan gesture actions resolve policy and apply through the viewport pan path;
- expected rejection actions assert the precise `ViewportGestureRejection` without mutating view
  state.

Adapter conformance now covers accepted scroll policy, accepted drag-pan policy, connection
rejection, and selection rejection.
